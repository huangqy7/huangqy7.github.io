Qianyi Huang



Email: huangqy@sustech.edu.cn

I am currently a research assistant professor at SUSTech. I received my Ph.D. degree in Department of Computer Science and Engineering from HKUST, where my Ph.D. advisor is Prof. Qian Zhang. After that, I worked at HKUST as a post-doc for one year. Before joining HKUST, I got my bachelor degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
